\underline{Proof.}

Let $X = \{ n | n \in \mathbb{N} \} \text{ and let } Y = \{ n+ 1/2n | n \in \mathbb{N} \}$. $X$ and $Y$ are closed and disjoint. 
By definition of distance between two sets,

\begin{center}
$d(X,Y) = inf\{ d(x,y) | x \in X \text{ and } y \in Y \}$
$ = inf \{ 1/2n \} = 0$. $\Box$
\end{center}
